MY SAAS

Meet Zarvalo.

A prospecting tool for local SEO agencies that need to find leads with weak SEO on their Google Maps profiles.

01

What it is

Zarvalo gives local SEO agencies qualified local businesses that need better Google Maps SEO. It also writes outreach emails for them and includes a lot of extra features around prospecting.

02

How it works

Enter a city and niche. Zarvalo does the research and gives you local businesses that most likely need, and will pay for, better SEO on their Google Maps profile.

03

Who it’s for

Local SEO agencies and solo founders who want to save time and money instead of spending hours on manual prospecting.

WHAT I BUILD

The parts of building I care about most.

Useful products

I want to build stuff people actually want. Not random ideas that sound smart, but tools that make a real task easier.

Clean systems

I like when things are simple, organized, and easy to improve. Clean systems make it easier to move fast without everything breaking.

Day-to-day help

The best software helps with normal daily work. Less manual searching, less guessing, fewer boring repetitive steps.

Real improvement

I care about getting better at the craft, not just shipping something and forgetting it. I keep learning, testing, and tightening the work.

How I think

Ship fast, learn faster

I release early and iterate often. Every version teaches me something the previous one couldn’t. Progress beats waiting for perfect.

Do fewer things, better

I’m not trying to build 10 products. I go deep on the ones that matter. Less breadth, more depth, better outcomes.

Think, then build

I focus on structure and user flow before touching code. Clear thinking and strong execution are what turn ideas into products people use.

Design is a function

Clean, intentional design isn’t decoration. It’s the difference between a user who stays and one who bounces in three seconds.

Founder mindset

I think about leverage, not just output. The goal is always to build something that works harder than the hours I put in.
