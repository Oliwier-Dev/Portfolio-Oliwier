MY SAAS

Meet Zarvalo.

A lead generation tool built for web agencies. Find local businesses with broken or outdated websites before your competitors do.

01

What it is

Zarvalo scrapes Google Maps, detects broken or outdated websites, and scores every lead 0–100 using real signals. No AI fluff.

02

How it works

Pick a city and niche. Zarvalo does the research. You get a ranked list of businesses most likely to pay for a redesign.

03

Who it’s for

Web agencies and solo founders who want a repeatable outreach pipeline without spending hours on manual research.
