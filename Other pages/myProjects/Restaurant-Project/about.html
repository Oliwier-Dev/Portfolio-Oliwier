Our Story

Founded in 1998, Vellaro began as a small family owned kitchen guided by clarity, warmth, and craft. Over time, it has grown into a destination known for precision, authenticity, and refined dining. Every dish reflects a dedication to carefully sourced ingredients, perfected technique, and lasting experience.

Where it All Began

Vellaro was founded in 1998 with a simple belief: that dining should feel intentional. In a small family kitchen, early days were shaped by long hours, careful preparation, and a deep respect for ingredients. There was no rush to expand, only a focus on doing things properly. Learning flavors, refining technique, and understanding the quiet discipline behind great food.

Craft Over Time

As the restaurant grew, so did its philosophy. Recipes evolved through repetition, patience, and attention to detail. Ingredients were chosen not for convenience, but for quality and origin. Each dish became a balance of tradition and restraint, where nothing was added without purpose and nothing was left to chance.

Today

Today, Vellaro stands as a reflection of that journey. Every plate carries the same values that defined the beginning: clarity, warmth, and precision. The space, the service, and the cuisine are designed to slow time, to create an experience that feels considered, memorable, and quietly exceptional.

Meet Chef Alessandro

With over 20 years of experience, Chef Alessandro blends tradition, innovation, and a passion for locally sourced ingredients in every dish. He balances classic techniques with creative flair, crafting plates that are expertly prepared, memorable, and full of character.
