Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 17, 2026

Summary

This website is a simple portfolio. I do not collect personal data for marketing or advertising. The only data saved locally is your theme preference (light or dark) so the site remembers your choice. The site also uses privacy-focused analytics and performance monitoring tools provided by Vercel.

What data we collect

I do not collect names, emails, or other directly identifiable personal information through this site.

The site stores a small value in your browser (localStorage) to remember whether you chose light or dark mode.

The site also uses Vercel Web Analytics and Vercel Speed Insights. These tools collect anonymized technical information such as page views, browser type, device type, approximate location (country level), and performance metrics. No cookies are used for analytics purposes.

How we use data

The theme preference is used only to improve your viewing experience.

Analytics and performance data are used to understand how the site is used and to improve performance, reliability, and user experience. This data is not sold or used for advertising.

Cookies and local storage

This site does not use tracking cookies. A single local storage entry remembers your theme preference.

Analytics provided by Vercel do not rely on cookies and are designed to be privacy-focused.

If you clear your browser data, your saved theme preference will be removed.
